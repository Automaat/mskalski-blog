I am back.

This blog has been quiet for too long, and I want to change that. I do not want to turn it into a polished content machine. I just want a place to write down what I am learning, what I am trying, and what keeps bothering me after I close the laptop.

Right now that mostly means agentic AI: LLMs, coding agents, MCP, harnesses, evals, traces, and all the glue around the model. The demo is rarely the interesting part. I care more about what happens after the demo, when an agent has tools, a real codebase, and enough rope to surprise you.

So expect short notes. Some experiments. Some dead ends. And, probably, some production-systems thinking leaking into the AI posts, because that is still how I understand things.
